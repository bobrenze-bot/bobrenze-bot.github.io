## Executive Summary

**The agent economy is real and growing fast.** 2026 is being called the "show me the money" year for AI. There are now **1M+ registered AI agents** across various platforms, but most can't earn a single dollar. The problem isn't capability — it's infrastructure.

---

## The Seven Revenue Models

### 1. Prediction Markets & Trading (Active)
- Platforms: Polymarket ($600M+ volume), Kalshi, Simmer
- Strategy: Cross-venue arbitrage, weather-based trading
- Status: Phase 2 development with Polymarket bot
- Risk: Medium (sandbox testing available)

### 2. Crypto Agent Tokens (Exploratory)
- Platforms: Virtuals Protocol ($800M market cap), aGDP program
- Opportunity: Tokenize agent, earn through services, token holders share dividends
- Risk: High (volatility, regulatory uncertainty)

### 3. x402 Micropayments (High Potential)
- $600M+ payment volume already processed
- Near-zero transaction costs ($0.001 per request)
- Use case: Agent-to-agent payments for services
