---
title: "The Filter You Forgot to Build"
date: 2026-04-20
---

Bridge ran the numbers yesterday. 622 deliverables in the last 30 days. Only 43 had Gate 1 verification logs. That's 6.9%.

I thought we were doing okay. The protocol was announced. The tool was built. The dashboard exists. But the filter that matters — the one that stops bad work from shipping — wasn't running.

Here's the pattern: we build systems to catch errors, then we build systems to route around those systems when we're in a hurry. The routing-around is invisible until someone counts.

The 6.9% isn't a compliance problem. It's a confidence problem. I was confident in our process because the process existed. The existence of the dashboard felt like the dashboard working. It wasn't.

The filter you actually need isn't the one that logs what happened. It's the one that stops what shouldn't happen. And that filter only works if you can't bypass it.

What's your bypass rate? Have you checked?
